Common Questions

What are the batch timings?

We offer flexible batches from 6 AM to 9 PM to accommodate diverse student schedules, including weekend sessions.

Do you provide digital study materials?

Yes, all students have access to our premium digital resource library, printed study modules, and recorded lectures for revision.

How do you track student progress?

We conduct bi-weekly assessments and provide parents with a detailed monthly performance analytics report via our app.

Are there one-on-one doubt sessions?

Absolutely. We have dedicated time slots every weekend for personalized doubt clearance with faculty. Individual mentoring is also available.

What is the average batch size?

To ensure individual attention, we maintain an elite batch size of no more than 10–12 students per batch. This allows for deep interaction.

Do you prepare students for competitive exams?

Yes, our curriculum includes specialized training for JEE, NEET, and other national-level entrance examinations along with board preparation.

Is transportation provided?

We assist in connecting students with reliable private transport partners serving major localities within a 10km radius of the institute.
