About Our Institute

Where Learning Meets Excellence

Gurukula Tuitions was founded with a single mission: to provide quality, affordable coaching that focuses on conceptual understanding rather than rote memorization. Over the past decade, we have helped hundreds of students in Tiruvallur achieve their academic goals and build strong foundations for their future careers.

  • Individualized attention for every learner.
  • Comprehensive coverage of CBSE & State Board syllabus.
  • Regular assessments and performance tracking.
  • Conducive and safe learning environment.

FAQs

Common Questions

Clear answers to help you make the best choice for your education.

What is the batch size at Gurukula?

To ensure individual attention, we maintain a strict batch size of maximum 15 students per group.

Do you provide demo classes?

Yes, we offer two free demo classes for all new students to experience our teaching methodology before enrolling.

How do you track student progress?

We conduct weekly tests and monthly assessments. Progress reports are shared with parents through regular monthly meetings.

What are the batch timings?

Batch timings are generally scheduled between 5:00 PM and 8:30 PM on weekdays, keeping school hours in mind.

Do you coach for both CBSE and State Board?

Yes, we have specialized study materials and teaching strategies for both CBSE and State Board curriculum.
